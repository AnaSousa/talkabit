MsC Fernanda Ledesma

Tema: "Programação para miúdos" (Coding by kids)

Percurso:

  • Informatics teacher from the 7th to 12th grades (basic school and high scool);
  • President of the national association of informatic teachers (Associação Nacional de Professores de Informática - ANPRI);
  • Director of the Teachers Formation Center (Centro de Formação de Professores);
  • Member of the Scientific Counsil of the Educational Evaluation Institute (Instituto de Avaliação Educativa - IAVE);
  • Master in E-learning systems management by the Nova University of Lisbon;
  • Received the award for Innovative Teacher by Microsoft in 2008, representing Portugal in the European Forum of Innovative Teachers;
  • Co-author of school books for the TIC (Communication and information technologies) subject of the publishers "Edições ASA" and the group "Editorial Leya".

Vítor Dinis

Theme: How to sell an IT company?

Biography:

  • Vitor is a co-founder Partner and CFO of Pathena. He serves as a Non-Executive Board Member of several of Pathena's portfolio companies. He's also one of the Directors of Pathena Fund SICAR (SCA). He holds a Master's Degree in Accounting and Business Management from Oporto Accounting Institute – Portugal. His career started at Arthur Andersen in 1997. In 2002 he joined Deloitte as a Manager. In 2003 he assumed the position of CFO at Enabler, a 30M€ systems integrator firm focused on Oracle retail implementations. During his professional career, Vitor completed continuous training in several areas such as general management, tax, leadership, company valuation and M&A in different schools including Universidade Católica. In 2006 Enabler was acquired by Wipro Technologies, a 5b $ company, Vitor took the role of Senior Manager of Internal Audit & Risk Management of Wipro’s European Geography.
