Fernanda Ledesma

Subject: Coding by kids

Biography:

  • Informatics teacher from the 7th to 12th grades (basic school and high scool);
  • President of the national association of informatic teachers (Associação Nacional de Professores de Informática - ANPRI);
  • Director of the Teachers Formation Center (Centro de Formação de Professores);
  • Member of the Scientific Counsil of the Educational Evaluation Institute (Instituto de Avaliação Educativa - IAVE);
  • Master in E-learning systems management by the Nova University of Lisbon;
  • Received the award for Innovative Teacher by Microsoft in 2008, representing Portugal in the European Forum of Innovative Teachers;
  • Co-author of school books for the TIC (Communication and information technologies) subject of the publishers "Edições ASA" and the group "Editorial Leya".

Note: Presentation to be conducted in Portuguese.

Vitor Dinis

Subject: How to sell an IT company?

Biography:

  • Vitor is a co-founder Partner and CFO of Pathena. He serves as a Non-Executive Board Member of several of Pathena's portfolio companies. He's also one of the Directors of Pathena Fund SICAR (SCA).
  • He holds a Master's Degree in Accounting and Business Management from Oporto Accounting Institute – Portugal;
  • His career started at Arthur Andersen in 1997;
  • In 2002 he joined Deloitte as a Manager;
  • In 2003 he assumed the position of CFO at Enabler, a 30M€ systems integrator firm focused on Oracle retail implementations;
  • During his professional career, Vitor completed continuous training in several areas such as general management, tax, leadership, company valuation and M&A in different schools including Universidade Católica;
  • In 2006 Enabler was acquired by Wipro Technologies, a 5b $ company, Vitor took the role of Senior Manager of Internal Audit & Risk Management of Wipro’s European Geography.

Note: Presentation to be conducted in Portuguese.

Pedro Veiga

Subject: Copyright law

Biography:

  • Pedro Veiga was born in 1952;
  • He has a degree in Electrical Engineering by the Instituto Superior Técnico, Technical University of Lisbon in 1975; a PhD in Electrical and Computers Engineering, by the Superior Technical Institute, Technical University of Lisbon in 1984; ""Agregação"" in Electrical and Computers Engineering, by the Superior Technical Institute, Technical University of Lisbon in 1992;
  • He was the pro-dean of the University of Lisbon, responsible for the Information Systems (2009-2013);
  • Between 1997-2013 he was both the president of FCCN (Foundation for the National Scientific Computation) and manager of the Portuguese Internet domain (.PT);
  • He's currently a professor, in the Informatic Department of the Faculty of Sciences of the Lisbon University, Founder and President of the Internet Society Portuguese chapter and Member of the Qualification and Admission Counsil of the Order of Engineers (""Ordem dos Engenheiros""), since 2010.

Note: Presentation to be conducted in Portuguese.

Guilherme Martins

Subject: Creative computation

Biography:

  • Guilherme Martins is an Artist and Technology Creator with heart and soul, holding more than 10 years of experience in Interaction Design and Video Art;
  • He specializes in the creation of visual contents for stage performances. Since 2007, his visual environments have been used in dance, opera, theater and performances and he has collaborated with renowned directors like Rui Horta, Tiago Guedes and Anatoly Praudin;
  • In the past has also worked as a visual artist for several internationally renowned companies, such as Ogilvy Interactive and YDreams;
  • In his tireless search for new forms of artistic expression, Guilherme became interested in robotics and what he would eventually call “Creative Robotics”;
  • ArticaCC was born in 2011, founded by him and André Almeida, with the intention of blending art and technology to create unique human experiences.

Note: Presentation to be conducted in Portuguese.
